Our Story
The founders of Les Pet Shop wanted to provide the most decadent treats, luxurious accessories and most comfortable beds so owners everywhere can spoil their beloved companions. On top of that, we here at Les Pets Shop sources our wide selection of animals, that ranges from cats, dogs to sugar gliders and iguanas from ethical breeders, so soon-to-be pet owners can rest easy knowing their pets did not come from breeding mills.
Why Us?
As mentoned earlier, we source our pets from ethical breeders and not breeding mills. This means that the code of ethics were closely followed when handling these animals.




What do we do?

Care for Animals

Here at Les Pet Shop we provide the best love and care for the animals we sell here, ensuring the best care and treatment possible.

Ensure ethical treeatment of animals

Pets here are well loved and bred ethically, so customers can rest easy knowing that the adopted pets were not from illegal mills.

Provide the best resources

We try our best to source the best of the best products for every animal. We even offer customized pet food so even pets with different dietary constraints can eat well.
